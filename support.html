Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: What is Unloque?
    A: It’s a free third-party app that lets you apply for government aid programs like 4Ps, DOST scholarships, faster, easier, and with better transparency. We’re here to make things more convenient for you, not to replace government systems.
  • Q: Who can use Unloque?
    A: Any Filipino citizen can use our services.
  • Q: Is Unloque a government app?
    A: Nope! It’s an independent platform built to support transparency and accessibility, and to help citizens access public services more easily.
  • Q: Is my personal data safe?
    A: Yes. Your data is encrypted and only accessible to authorized personnel from government agencies.
  • Q: How do I know if my application was received?
    A: After submission, you’ll see your application status under the “My Applications” tab. You’ll also receive app notifications when there are updates.
  • Q: What if I submitted the wrong document?
    A: You can update your application before it’s reviewed. If it’s already being processed, wait for the rejection notice and reapply with the correct files.
  • Q: Can I apply for more than one program?
    A: Yes! You can apply to any programs you’re eligible for.
